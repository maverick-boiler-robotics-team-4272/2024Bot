OTFPF = On The Fly Path Follow

Driver Controls:
    A: Face Speaker
    B: Reset heading (set current face direction to forwards)
    X: OTFPF
    Y: Reset odometry (Limelight)

    Left Bumper: Auto Aim
    Right Bumper: Shoot

    Left Stick: Unassigned
    Right Stick: Unassigned

    Back: Reset modules (Reset module rotation based on mavcoders)
    Start: Unassigned

    D-Pad: OTFPF selection

    Left Trigger: Unassigned
    Right Trigger: Unassigned

    Left Joystick: Drivetrain Translation
    Right Joystick: Drivetrian rotation


Operator Controls:
    A: Amp Outfeed
    B: Zero Elevator
    X: Unassigned
    Y: Reset odometry (Limelight)

    Left Bumper: Rev
    Right Bumper: Amp Setpoint (For use while climbing)

    Left Stick Click: Unassigned
    Right Stick Click: Unassigned

    Back: Unassigned
    Start: Unassigned

    D-Pad: Arm Elevator Setpoints
        Up: Amp Setpoint
        Right: Trap Setpoint
        Down: Pre Climb Setpoint
        Left: White Line Setpoint


    Left Trigger: Feed and Intake
    Right Trigger: Outfeed and Outtake

    Left Joystick: (Up/Down) Climber
